What I Actually Do

What You're Hiring Me For

Most ad accounts waste 30–40% of budget on audiences that don't convert, tracking that misfires, and creatives that never get tested. I fix that — then build the system that keeps it fixed.

Paid Acquisition

I structure campaigns around your unit economics — audience segmentation, bid strategy, and budget allocation that tie directly to CAC targets. Not set-and-forget. Active management.

Tracking & Attribution

If your data is wrong, every decision downstream is wrong. I implement GA4, GTM, and Meta CAPI so you know exactly which channel, ad, and audience is generating revenue — not just clicks.

Testing & Optimisation

Every test has a written hypothesis and defined win criteria before it runs. I track results in a shared doc. You know what was tested, what won, and why — not just "we tried some things."

CAC & ROAS Management

I look at CAC, ROAS, and payback period — not CPM or CTR in isolation. When a campaign is underperforming I can tell you exactly which variable is the problem and what I'm changing.

Process

How an Engagement Works

From first call to live campaigns, here's what the process looks like — no ambiguity.

01

Audit & Diagnose

I go through your ad accounts, analytics setup, and conversion data. I'm looking for wasted spend, broken tracking, and the one or two levers most likely to move CAC.

02

Build the System

Based on the audit, I map out which channels to run, how to structure campaigns, what to track, and how to report. You sign off before anything goes live.

03

Launch & Test

Small budget, clear hypothesis, defined win criteria. I don't scale spend until there's evidence of what's working — and I document every test result.

04

Optimise & Scale

Once we have winners, I scale them and cut what's not performing. Every decision is logged so nothing gets lost when the campaign grows.

05

Report & Align

You get a live Looker Studio dashboard and a weekly summary. No waiting for end-of-month reports to know if things are working.
