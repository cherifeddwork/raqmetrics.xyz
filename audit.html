What gets reviewed

Not a surface-level checklist. Every item maps to a real failure mode found across hundreds of accounts.

01
Campaign Structure & Account Hygiene
Campaign objectives, budget hierarchy, consolidation vs. fragmentation, naming consistency, test vs. live separation, and bid strategy alignment to funnel stage.
Fragmented structure is the #1 cause of learning phase lockout
02
Audience Segmentation & Overlap
Audience size vs. budget, overlap between adsets, cold vs. warm vs. retargeting separation, exclusions applied correctly, custom audience freshness and match rates.
30–40% of budget lost to adsets bidding against themselves
03
Creative Performance & Fatigue Signals
Frequency per placement, CTR trend over time, creative diversity by format and angle, fatigue threshold by audience size, and rotation cadence vs. actual performance data.
Most accounts rotate creative on feeling, not on signal
04
Tracking Accuracy — Pixel, CAPI & UTMs
Pixel firing status, duplicate event detection, CAPI event match quality score, UTM consistency across all ads, GA4 and platform data reconciliation, and attribution window settings.
7 in 10 accounts have at least one broken conversion signal
05
Budget Allocation & Bid Strategy
Channel split vs. funnel stage, prospecting/retargeting ratio, CBO vs. ABO logic, spend pacing, daily vs. lifetime budget tradeoffs, and bid cap vs. cost cap application.
Most accounts underinvest in cold and over-retarget warm audiences
06
Attribution Model & Reporting Gaps
Platform-reported vs. actual revenue reconciliation, attribution window vs. sales cycle mismatch, view-through vs. click-through breakdown, and channel overlap causing double-counting.
Attribution mismatch is the most common reason CAC looks lower than it is
